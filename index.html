Frontend

This is the latest chapter of my life. I started the Frontend Developer program at Hyper Island in august 2019 and have since been hard at work learning the ways of web development, user experience and agile working environments.

I started this journey because I found myself becoming gradually more interested in the technology side of things in my work. Almost everything today has a digital component to it and requires specific knowledge in regards to us as humans and technology.

UX / UI

UX is not a far step away from the user centered design methods we practice in modern times industrial design and user centered design has always been one of the most important factors in any development project that I have been a part of.

As someone who has been playing video games for most of his life, UI is the area I’m currently the most passionate about. I’m thrilled with the opportunity to develop this skill at the Frontend Program at Hyper Island as it really becomes a focal point in the blend between great frontend and great design.

Design

I enjoy making beautiful things and to me that beauty can take many shapes, be it well structured code, a satisfying css animation on a button hover or an aesthetically pleasing physical product.

I realised that similarly to when you create something physical when you have to be aware of the constraints of production, that same thing is true for when you create something digital. To a designer it is important that the resulting product, be it digital or physical, holds true to the intended design that was passed on to production or development.

This will only happen if the designer has the knowledge to produce a design that is feasible and easily communicated.
